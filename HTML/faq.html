General Questions :

Having trouble logging in?

Don't worry, this happens to everyone. If you forget your password, click 'Forgot? ' above the sign in box, then enter the email address you use for your karir.com account. Click 'Send' and we will send your password to your email. Be sure to check your spam/junk mail if you can't find the email in your inbox.

How do I change my password?

First, log in to your karir.com account. Go to your profile icon at the top right of the page. Click "Manage Account" which is shaped like a gear symbol. Fill in the fields and cross your data.

How to register on JalankKarirmu.com?

Go to JalanKarirmu.com main page and fill in the required fields in the sign up box and click. After you have submitted the information, please check your email to activate your account. Make sure to check your spam/junk mail if you can't find the confirmation email in your inbox. After you have activated your account, when you first log in, please fill in the required basic information. Next, you can write your resume by filling in the available fields. Writing your complete resume is very important to increase your chances for a career. Make sure your resume is complete and up to date.

Who sees my resume?

When you create your resume, all Employers will be able to see your resume by default. However, only prospective companies can see your contact information.

Why didn't I get a response after applying online?

Every company has its own method of evaluating resumes. Some Companies may send automated email replies or call you to respond to your application. However, there are Companies that will not contact you unless they want to start the interview process.

Career Faq :

How can I increase my chances of being recruited?

Create a resume that really stands out. You want to highlight your specific experience and role so the company knows you will be a good fit for their needs. Adding more experience, education, certifications and skills would go a long way.

How do I submit a job vacancy?

First, you will need to register as an Employer. As we are currently in the process of improving our products and services, our Business Consultants will assist you in posting career opportunities on our website. Please call +021 102 001 to speak with our Business Consultants.
